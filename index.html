Why Dropwise?

Four ways Dropwise makes a difference — for your garden and the planet.

Save Water

Data-driven schedules based on local weather, soil type, and plant species mean you never overwater again. Water usage drops by up to 40%.

Save Money

Less water means lower utility bills. The average home garden saves over $200 per year just by watering at the right time, in the right amount.

Save the Environment

Overwatering causes runoff that carries fertilizers and pesticides into waterways. Precise watering protects local ecosystems and conserves a precious resource.

Save the Climate

Pumping, treating, and heating water consumes energy and produces CO₂. Every gallon saved reduces your carbon footprint. Small changes, big impact.

How It Works

Three simple steps to smarter watering. No hardware, no installation — just your browser.

1

Enter Your Garden

Tell Dropwise about your plants, soil type, and garden size. It automatically pulls hyperlocal weather data for your area — no sensors needed.

2

Get Your Schedule

Dropwise calculates the optimal watering schedule based on plant needs, soil moisture, upcoming rainfall, temperature, and evaporation rates. Updated daily.

3

Water & Track

Follow the daily plan. Mark tasks as done and track your water savings over time. Watch your garden thrive while your water bill shrinks — week after week.
